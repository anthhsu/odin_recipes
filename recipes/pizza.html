Pizza


This is a recipe for pizza.

This recipe is the finest pizza recipe in the world. By the end of it, your guests will be wishing they had another slice to eat! Get ready to make the best pizza of your life.

Ingredients:

  • Flour
  • Water
  • Yeast
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Pepperoni
  • Mushroom

Steps:

  1. First, prepare the dough. Mix flour, water, and yeast. Let it rise.
  2. Next, roll out the dough into a flat, circular shape.
  3. Then, add tomato sauce and your desired ingredients.
  4. Finally, bake at 500 degrees for 15 minutes.
