Guacamole

This is a recipe for guacamole.

This guacamole is the perfect blend of salt, lime, and of course, avocado. You'll never go to Chipotle again.

Ingredients:

  • Avocado
  • Onion
  • Cilantro
  • Jalapeño
  • Lime
  • Salt
  • Queso Fresco

Steps:

  1. In a molcajete or mortar & pestle, combine onion, jalapeño, and cilantro. Add a large pinch of salt.
  2. Pound until ingredients are well crushed and a wet paste is created.
  3. Add avocado and pound until mashed.
  4. Squeeze in lime juice, and stir to combine.
  5. Add in some more salt to taste, and optionally top with queso fresco. Enjoy!
