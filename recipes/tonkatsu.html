Tonkatsu

This is a recipe for tonkatsu.

Tonkatsu is a Japanese dish, also known as pork cutlet. It is lightly breaded and fried, and served with a special tonkatsu sauce. It is often served with a cabbage salad and rice.

Ingredients:

  • Pork Loin Chop
  • Flour
  • Egg
  • Panko
  • Salt
  • Canola Oil
  • Cabbage
  • Tonkatsu Sauce (Bull-Dog brand)

Steps:

  1. Season pork chops with salt ahead of time (a few hours to overnight).
  2. Tenderize / evenly thin out pork chops.
  3. Cover with flour, egg, panko.
  4. Fry in oil heated to 350Â° F.
  5. Serve with shredded cabbage and rice.
